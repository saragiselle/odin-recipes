Vori Vori

What is Vori Vori

Vori Vori is a thick, yellow soup with little balls made of courn flour and cheese, can be cooked with chicken or beef.

It's a traditional food from Paraguay.

Ingredients

For the soup:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp coarse salt
  • oregano
  • 16 cups (4 liters) of boiling water

For the Vori Vori Balls

  • 2 cups corn flour
  • 1 thik slice of fresh cheese
  • Bone Broth to moisten the mixture

Preparation

  1. Brown the chicken in its fat, without burning.
  2. Remove from heat.
  3. Prepare the pan with boiling water.
  4. Put all the vegetables, sautéing them lightly in the fat left in the pan in which the chicken was sautéed.
  5. Add the sautéed vegetables and the browned chicken to the boiling water. Salt and add the shredded oregano. Boil over medium heat until soft.
  6. Add the vorí-vorí and sprinkle with a tablespoon of cornmeal. Cook 10 minutes more. Yields 6 servings.
  7. Place cornmeal and shredded cheese in a deep bowl.
  8. Moisten with the hot broth until you obtain a dough with which you can form small balls the size of grapes. Pour them little by little into the boiling broth.
  9. Boil for 5 minutes.
  10. If the broth is too thick, add boiling water.
